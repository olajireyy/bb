Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

What is CyberSense Hub?

CyberSense Hub is a cybersecurity platform designed to protect Nigerian students, small businesses, and campus communities from online scams, fraud, and cyber threats. We use AI-powered detection to analyze suspicious messages, links, and payment alerts in real-time.

How do I create an account?

1. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up" on the login page
2. Enter your name, email, and phone number
3. Create a strong password
4. Select your user type (Student, Business Owner, or Institution)
5. Click "Sign Up" to complete registration

Is CyberSense Hub free to use?

Yes! CyberSense Hub is completely free for students and campus communities. We offer basic features at no cost. Premium features for businesses may be available in the future.

Scanning & Detection

How do I scan a suspicious message?

1. Go to the Scan page
2. Select the "Text" tab
3. Copy and paste the suspicious message
4. Click "Scan Now"
5. Review the results and risk level

What types of threats can CyberSense Hub detect?

We detect multiple threat types including:
• Phishing emails and messages
• Fake scholarship scams
• Fraudulent job offers
• Investment scams (Forex, MMM, etc.)
• Fake bank transfer alerts
• Malicious URLs and links
• Impersonation attempts
• Data harvesting schemes

How accurate is the threat detection?

Our detection system uses pattern matching algorithms specifically trained on Nigerian scam patterns. While highly accurate, we recommend treating all high-risk alerts seriously and verifying through official channels when in doubt.

Can I scan images?

Yes! You can upload screenshots of suspicious messages or bank alerts. Our OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology extracts text from images and analyzes them for threats.

Payment Verification

How do I verify a bank alert?

1. Go to "Verify Payment" from the dashboard
2. Upload a screenshot of the bank alert
3. Click "Verify Payment"
4. Wait for OCR analysis
5. Review the verification results

What makes a bank alert fake?

Red flags include:
• Wrong or missing bank name
• Inconsistent font styling
• Missing transaction reference
• Unusual timestamp format
• Poor image quality or editing signs
• Spelling/grammar errors

Should I release goods after verification?

Always verify through multiple channels:
1. Check our verification results
2. Call your bank directly to confirm
3. Check your actual account balance
4. Never rely on screenshots alone

Reporting & Campus Safety

How do I report a security incident?

1. Go to Campus Reports
2. Click "Submit New Report"
3. Fill in incident details
4. Upload evidence (optional)
5. Submit anonymously or with your name
6. Click "Submit Report"

Can I report anonymously?

Yes! You can check the "Submit anonymously" option when filing a report. Your identity will not be recorded or shared with anyone.

What happens after I submit a report?

1. Report is immediately logged
2. Security team reviews within 24 hours
3. Investigation begins if needed
4. Campus community may be alerted
5. You receive updates (if not anonymous)

Account & Settings

How do I change my password?

1. Go to Settings from the menu
2. Click "Change Password" in Security section
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (min 8 characters)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click "Update Password"

How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Settings
2. Scroll to "Data & Storage"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm by typing "DELETE"
Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone!
