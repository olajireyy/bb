Why We Built CyberSense Hub

Nigeria has one of the highest rates of cybercrime in Africa, with students and small businesses being the most vulnerable targets. Every day, thousands of Nigerians fall victim to:

  • Fake scholarship scams targeting students
  • Fraudulent job offers with upfront fees
  • Fake bank transfer alerts used to defraud vendors
  • Investment scams (MMM, Forex, etc.)
  • Phishing attacks and identity theft

We created CyberSense Hub to democratize cybersecurity protection. Our platform uses locally-trained algorithms that understand Nigerian scam patterns, making protection accessible to everyone—regardless of technical expertise or financial resources.
